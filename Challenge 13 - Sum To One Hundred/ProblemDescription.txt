Programming Challenge: Sum To One Hundred

Copied from Five programming problems every Software Engineer should be able to solve in less than 1 hour (problem 5): https://blog.svpino.com/2015/05/07/five-programming-problems-every-software-engineer-should-be-able-to-solve-in-less-than-1-hour

Write a program that outputs all possibilities to put + or - or nothing between the numbers 1, 2, ..., 9 (in this order) such that the result is always 100.

For example: 1 + 2 + 34 – 5 + 67 – 8 + 9 = 100.
